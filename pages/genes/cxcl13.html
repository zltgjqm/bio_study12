CXCL13

C-X-C motif chemokine ligand 13 — a B-lymphocyte chemoattractant that organizes follicular structures in lymphoid and tertiary lymphoid tissue.

Known function

Recruits B cells and T follicular helper cells via CXCR5, driving the formation of germinal-center-like structures. Elevated CXCL13 signaling is a recurring marker of tertiary lymphoid structure (TLS) formation outside canonical lymphoid organs.

Associated cell types

Macrophage Follicular dendritic cell T follicular helper cell

Associated tissues

Lung Lymph node Synovium
